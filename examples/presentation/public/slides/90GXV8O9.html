Chapter 3: Shared Leadership

GOVERNANCE RIGHTS BY OWNERSHIP

Structured Framework for Multi-Principal Control

CH3-S04

33%+

Major Decision Veto

Right to block fundamental company changes (mergers, debt, dissolution).

20-33%

Voting Board Seat

Direct vote on strategic direction, budgets, and executive compensation.

10-20%

Board Observer

Right to attend meetings and receive all reports, but no binding vote.

0-10%

Profit Sharing Only

Economic participation in distributions; no governance authority.

Additional Terms

VESTING SCHEDULE 3-5 YEARS

Ownership rights phase in over time to ensure long-term retention. Departure before cliff forfeits unvested equity.

REPURCHASE RIGHTS MANDATORY

Company holds right of first refusal to buy back shares if a principal leaves, ensuring control stays internal.

"Governance scales with commitment. This protects both the founder and the new principals."
