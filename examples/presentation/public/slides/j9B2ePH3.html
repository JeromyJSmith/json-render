UNDERSTANDING GIFT VS PURCHASE

Two Mechanisms for Acquiring Equity

CH2-S07

vs

GIFTED SHARES

  • No cash cost to Bodie (Luke transfers ownership for $0)

  • Luke incurs gift tax exposure on the transfer value

  • Vests immediately, no claw-back provisions

  • Builds ownership percentage without requiring capital

PURCHASED SHARES

  • Bodie pays Fair Market Value (FMV)

  • Requires Bodie to take on debt/cash outlay (SBA Loan)

  • Directly tied to loan repayment schedule

  • Demonstrates meaningful "skin in the game"

Why Use Both?

A balanced mix limits Luke's immediate tax exposure while ensuring Bodie has a real financial commitment to the firm's success.
