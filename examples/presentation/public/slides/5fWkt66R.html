Chapter 2: Solo Principal Path

SOLO PATH: PROS VS CONS

SCENARIO A EVALUATION

VS

The Upside

STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES

Clear Succession Line

One decision-maker simplifies the transition. No ambiguity about who holds final authority.

Simpler Governance

Avoids complex multi-partner voting structures, deadlock risks, and operating agreements.

Maximum Ownership

Direct path to 49% equity stake, capturing the largest share of future profits personally.

The Downside

RISK FACTORS

Highest Capital Requirement

~$8.3M total investment burden falls 100% on one person's balance sheet.

Concentrated Risk

Single point of failure. If you can't work, the business (and your debt service) stops.

No Construction Integration

Misses the opportunity to capture high-margin build revenue, limiting EBITDA growth.

Verdict: Achievable, but high-risk with capped upside.
